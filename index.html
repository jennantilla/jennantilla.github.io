About

Ex-Anthropologist who traded cultural ethnographies for software repositories.

I'm fascinated by code and the people who write it. Whether maintaining existing infrastructure or creating new systems from scratch, my non-traditional path to engineering allows me to see the human elements behind software.


After more than 5 years building teams and workplace experience at innovative Bay Area tech companies, I now feel called to contribute to actually building the products that make these companies successful.


When I'm not coding you might find me running, crafting, or thrifting. I've lived my whole life between California and Hawaii, so there is a good chance I'm at or near the ocean most of the time. Still trying to figure out that ideal beach workplace setup, though.
